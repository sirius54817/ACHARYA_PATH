Frequently Asked Questions

Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Register" button in the top right corner of any page. Fill out the registration form with your name, email, and password. Once submitted, you'll receive a confirmation email. Click the link in the email to verify your account, and you're all set to start using AChart Path.

Yes, most of the courses on AChart Path are completely free. We believe in making education accessible to everyone. However, some specialized courses or certification programs may have a nominal fee. These premium courses are clearly marked, and you'll always know before enrolling if there's any cost involved.

To apply for freelance jobs, you need to be logged into your account. Browse the jobs on our Freelance page and click "Apply" on any job that interests you. You'll be prompted to complete an application form which typically includes questions about your experience, portfolio, and proposed rate. Your application will then be sent to the client, who will contact you directly if they're interested in your services.

Yes, registered users can post their own events. Navigate to the Events page and click on "Add Your Event" button. Fill out the event details form, including title, date, location, description, and optionally upload an image. Your event will be reviewed by our team and, once approved, will appear in the events listing for others to discover.

After completing all modules and passing the final assessment of a course, you'll automatically be eligible for a certificate. Go to your user dashboard, navigate to "My Courses," and click on "Get Certificate" next to the completed course. You can download the certificate as a PDF or share it directly to your LinkedIn profile or other social media platforms.
